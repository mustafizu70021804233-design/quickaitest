CHECKLIST FOR PLAY CONSOLE
======================================================
1. Upload your App Bundle: `app-release.aab` (found in `android/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/`)
2. App Icon: Upload `app_icon_512.png` (found in this folder)
3. Feature Graphic: Upload `feature_graphic.png` (found in this folder)
4. Privacy Policy: Host `privacy_policy.html` on a free site (like GitHub Pages, Google Sites, or a free Blogger) and paste the URL into the "App Content" -> "Privacy Policy" section.
5. Screenshots: 
   - Since the Play Console requires screenshots of the app running, simply install the `app-release.apk` on your own Android phone, open the app, and take 2-4 screenshots (e.g., the Start Screen, a Question, and the Result Screen). Transfer those to your computer and upload them directly.
